Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy SOPREME™? ▾
SOPREME™ is available at Whole Foods, Sprouts, HEB, Gelson's, and on Amazon. Use our Store Locator to find the nearest location near you.
What is the shelf life of SOPREME™ products? ▾
All SOPREME™ products are shelf-stable and have a best-by date printed on each can, bottle, or pouch. Store in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate after opening.
Do you ship internationally? ▾
Currently we ship within the United States only. We're working on expanding to additional markets. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know.
Are your products non-GMO and Kosher? ▾
Yes — all SOPREME™ products are Non-GMO certified and Kosher certified. We are also manufactured in an FSSC 22000 certified facility and lab tested for highest quality control.
How do I track my order? ▾
Once your order ships you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. If you haven't received it within 48 hours, check your spam folder or contact us below.
